Hot Chocolate

Enjoy this hot Chocolate on a nice rainy, or cold day!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 3 Cups Soy Milk

Steps

  1. Bring water, sugar, and cocoa to a boil in a small saucepan.
  2. Pour in soy milk and stir until combined and heated through, about 3 minutes.
